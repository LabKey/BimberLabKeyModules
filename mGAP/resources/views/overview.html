The Macaque Genotype and Phenotype Database (mGAP) provides access to genotype data collected on a large, pedigreed, rhesus macaque colony housed at the Oregon National Primate Research Center (ONPRC), ultimately including data from thousands of animals. The initial data release of mGAP includes a catalog of DNA sequence variants generated from whole genome sequencing (WGS) of 54 rhesus macaques. The genotype data set, along with clinical and phenotypic measures on the same animals, will be updated periodically.

Resources provided by mGAP:

  • Rhesus Macaque Variant Catalog: mGAP provides a curated and annotated list of macaque SNVs, generated from our cohort
  • Raw Sequence Data: whole genome and genotyping-by-sequencing (GBS) data are available for all animals
  • Genotype Data: genotypes will be available for all animals in Fall 2017
  • Phenotypic and Clinical Data: will be available in 2018

mGAP is supported by NIH R24OD021324. Please remember to cite this funding source in all publications that make use of mGAP data.
